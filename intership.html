Internship

We are one of the very few organizations in Spain who guarantees paid summer internships for our students, helping them to make the first step towards their professional life and successfully complete their university projects.

They will be given the chance to visit great places, meet lots of people and improve any foreign languages you speak. Skills development may include commercial awareness, strengthening communication skills as well as your customer service and sales skills. Being able to work abroad shows high levels of flexibility and adaptability.


Primary location
Spain

Spanish market is very open for international students, so there is a big diversity with the offers and locations.

Dominating sectors: Tourism Marketing and Customer Care.

  • Hotels:
    • €200 per month + accommodation + food
    • Duration: Minimum 3 months
  • Companies:
    • €400-500 per month
    • Duration: Minimum 6 months


France

France has a big demand for international students to work in their Hotels during summer time (Seaside) and winter (Alps).Most students need to have good level of French in order to find an internship.

  • Hotels:
    • € 400 per month + accommodation + food
    • Duration: Minimum 3 months


Greece

Tourism sector is very developed in Greece, Hoteliers are looking for students who can help them during summer season. They accept only Tourism students with English language (Russian appreciated).

  • Hotels:
    • € 400 per month + accommodation + food
    • Duration: Minimum 4 months


Mexico

Mexico offers internships in luxury hotels in safe zone with beautiful beach –Cancun. They require English and Spanish intermediate. Internship in Mexico requires visa. EU citizens can do an internship with Touristic visa for 3 months.

  • Hotels:
    • Accommodation + food
    • Duration: Minimum 3 months


China

All the luxury Hotels are looking ofr European students to improve the international image. They require education in Tourism, English language. Internship in China requires visa. Hotels usually assist students with application.

  • Hotels:
    • Salay + accommodation + food
    • Duration: Minimum 6 months
